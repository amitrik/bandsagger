The Game

Experience 18 holes of championship level golf on our excellent grounds.
All tee box and hole-related information can be supplemented on the green diagram.
Please reference the ruleset listed below.

  • Tee Boxes

    There are four tee boxes, denoted by blue diamonds labeled #1-4. They are located approximately at the four corners of the practice green.

  • Holes

    There are five holes, denoted by the holes labeled #1-5. They are approximated roughly to their locations on the practice green.

  • Architecture

    Each hole on the course, as listed on the diagram page, is a combination of a [TEE BOX #, HOLE #] pair.

  • Playability

    The golfer will start at the designated tee box and putt to the designated hole, 18 times.
